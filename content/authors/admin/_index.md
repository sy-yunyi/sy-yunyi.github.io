## About Me

Yunyi Zhang is a Ph.D. student as a member of joint Ph.D. program between National University of Defense Technology (advised by Professors Min Zhang) and Tsinghua University (advised by Professors Haixin Duan and Baojun Liu). His research interests include network security, internet measurement, and emerging cyberspace security threats. He has published papers at top academic venues, including USENIX Security, ACM IMC, ISOC NDSS, IEEE TDSC and IEEE TDSC. His academic research results have achieved real-world impact by receiving bonus from several vendors, including Baidu, Tencent, Micrcsoft, Yahoo, Alibaba and Amazon.
