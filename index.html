Yunyi Zhang

Postdoctoral Researcher

Network and Information Security Laboratory
Tsinghua University
Beijing, China

Yunyi Zhang is a postdoctoral researcher working under the supervision of Associate Professor Baojun Liu. His research interests include network security, internet measurement, emerging cyberspace security threats and LLM Privacy and Security. He has published papers at top academic venues, including USENIX Security, ACM CCS, ACM IMC, ISOC NDSS, IEEE TDSC and IEEE TIFS. His academic research results have achieved real-world impact by receiving bonus from several vendors, including Baidu, Tencent, Microsoft, Yahoo, Alibaba and Amazon, etc.

Research Interests

  • Network Security
  • DNS Security
  • Detection and Countermeasures of Cybercrime
  • LLM Privacy and Security
